Okay, I get it. Enough background. How do I get really big scores in Mario Kart Tour? The answer is… little by little. Your course score is affected by many elements, including things both inside and outside the actual race. All of these affect your final score. It may take some time to figure out how to get a lot of points in one race, but here are some tips for improving your scores:

Outside Race elements:

Raise your Base Stats to the max! If you cap out, use a points-cap ticket to go even further.

Any Favorite Characters, Karts or Gliders that have a skill level of 2 or higher get their base stat multiplied. Anything with an enormous level gets a ton of points like this.

Racing at a faster CC grants you more points for winning. Additionally, the CPU characters race better and are harder to defeat at faster CCs too.

Your player level increases as you win races. The placement score you win at the end of a race is affected by how high your player level is.

Inside Race Elements:

Press down on “2” and release on “GO!” for the best rocket starts.

All these actions grant a few points at a time, but they really do make a big difference in the end

Collecting Coins and Mushrooms will give you a bunch of Bonus points

Taking out your opponents grants a lot of bonus points. Additionally, Battle mode has a “Well Done!” bonus if you manage to win the battle within 90 seconds.

Things like pumpkins, Goombas, Rocky Wrenches, etc. give you points. Get creative with the course elements to boost your scores.

Obviously, placing 1st will get you the biggest reward. Additionally, having a higher player level increases the reward you get!

Combos increase the amount of points you get after performing actions during a race. Getting a big string of combos means getting a bunch of additional bonus points.
